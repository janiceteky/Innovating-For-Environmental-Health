Wet vacuuming or hot water extraction may work on items such as carpets or drapes. When dry, they should be dry-vacuumed, using a vacuum with multi-stage filtration and a HEPA filter or high filtration disk. Any porous items that are washed must be dried; if fast drying is not possible, the items should be thrown out. Once ceiling tiles and insulation get wet, it is easier to replace them.
